Label Submissions

You can send us your beat(s), EP or Album that you want to be reviewed for Label release, according to the rules below.


  1. Email the beats to noirelofi@gmail.com
  2. Email Subject: Demo Submission
  3. Beat name must be in "Artist Name - Song Title.wav" form
  4. Master volume should be in the range of -12 and -14 lufs
  5. The beats must be in the wav file format
  6. The beats must be previously unreleased

ABOUT US

Noire Lo-fi Records is a label with a “collective” spirit that has existed in Music Industry for years and was created by Independent Artist who took part in both the vocal and beat.

In general, they want to work in the field of lo-fi hip-hop beats, chill beats and boom bap beats.

Our aim is to reach talented beatmakers and artists who remain in the called “underground” parts of hip-hop culture and make good music heard.

Welcome to our Fam.
